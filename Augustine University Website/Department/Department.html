Dr. D. OLANIYI

ABORISADE

MEET THE HEAD OF DEPARTMENT

HEAD OF DEPARTMENT, FACULTY OF COMPUTING

Dr. D. Olaniyi Aborisade is a PhD-holder in Computer Science with over a decade of teaching and research experience. He earned his degrees from FUNAAB, the University of Ibadan, and completed doctoral benchwork at Hochschule Furtwangen University, Germany. An active professional, he is a member of the CPN and NCS, having served as the NCS Ogun State Vice Chairman from 2017 to 2019.

His research focuses on Cloud Information Security, Digital Forensics, and his current project, "A DNA-Based Model for Speaker Identification." With approximately 20 publications, he teaches core courses like Database Design, Operating Systems, and Programming. Currently, he serves as the Head of Department (HOD) for the Faculty of Computing at Augustine University.
