PROFFESSOR ZACHEUSS

OMOGBADEGUN

MEET THE DEAN

DEAN OF THE FACULTY OF COMPUTING

Zacchaeus Oni Omogbadegun holds BSc (Computer Science, Second Class Upper Div Hons, University of Ibadan, Ibadan, Nigeria, July 1979), MSc (CompSc, University of Lagos, Lagos, Nigeria, November 2003), and PhD (Computer Science, Covenant University, Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria, August 2012).

He has over thirty years of progressively cognate experience in Information Technology spanning Software Engineering, Education and Training. Worked either as an employee or Information Technology Consultant in bluechip industrial organizations within and outside Nigeria.

DR. A. L.

KOLAPO

MEET THE SUBDEAN

SUB DEAN OF THE FACULTY OF COMPUTING

Dr. A.L. Kolapo is an outstanding academician and key pillar of administration across computing portfolios. Serving as the Sub-Dean of the Faculty of Computing, he drives rigorous laboratory research setups, multi-faceted projects, and industry-focused curriculum frameworks.

His primary research focuses on functional bio-resource development, structural development of computational frameworks, and physical science modeling. He actively coordinates science mentorship networks and drives structured undergraduate internship schemes.

About Augustine University

Augustine University (AUI) is a private Catholic University located in the serene environment of Ilara-Epe, Lagos State. Approved by the Federal Executive Council of Nigeria in February 2015, the university officially commenced academic operations in October 2015.

AUI is committed to delivering premium training that incorporates the best modern tools, standard physical environments, scientific resources, and character-driven mentorship programs designed to yield highly competitive graduates.

Under our computing faculty, students utilize leading lab setups, automated software development pipelines, agile engineering workflows, and specialized research to stay ahead of world market transformations.
